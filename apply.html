Bootcamp FAQS

  • Absolutely! We understand that investing in your professional development is a significant decision, and we want to make it as accessible as possible. Therefore, we offer three interest-free installments to spread the cost of the program over time.

    The total program cost is $750, and you can choose to pay the installments according to the following schedule:
    Installment 1: $250 upon acceptance into the program.
    Installment 2: $250 due halfway through the program.
    Installment 3: $250 due at the program's completion.

  • For the bootcamp, you'll ultimately get to work out a schedule that works with the rest of your team. We do suggest a minimum time commitment of at least 15 - 20 hours per week.

  • By the end of the bootcamp, you'll have a solid understanding of the product development lifecycle and the skills needed to be a successful product manager.
    You'll also have a portfolio of real-world projects to showcase your skills to potential employers. You'll also have a network of product managers and mentors to help you grow in your career.

  • You'll need a laptop and a strong internet to succeed in the program. Don't worry, we'll provide you all the premium tools you need.

  • We have amazing real-life projects. But, you can also tell us! Learners decide for themselves too what they build (and whether they end up building something at all!). You work collaboratively as a team to find actual users, and determine actual customer pain points - just like in a real workplace.

  • To make sure you and your team stay on track, your team will be asked to submit things such as:
    (1) A spec document
    (2) Lo-fi and high-fi designs
    (3) A demo video + associated URLs
    (4) A blog writeup on your team experience and product - your Product Pack

  • You’ll be working with other like-minded, motivated techies looking to grow in their careers, across Design and Engineering. You’ll be navigating the Sprintleaps journey together! Our matching is determined based on teamwork styles, experiences, interests, and other logistics.
    Please note that Sprintleaps is a team-based learning program, and working with new people can be hard. That's part of the experience you're signing up for, so please make sure you're up for it.

  • Not everyone is used to hands-on, project-based, collaborative learning. It requires you to take initiative, display leadership, and communicate effectively on top of sharpening your technical skills. It's hard - and that's exactly why it works in helping you stand out and build credibility.

  • We offer career support by helping you build a portfolio of real-world projects. We'll also help you with your resume and LinkedIn profile. We'll also provide career advice and help you prepare for interviews.
