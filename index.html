Frequently Asked Questions

  • Sprintleap is a product management bootcamp that helps you learn product management by building real-world products with a mentor. Students are paired with a team of engineers, designers, and a senior PM mentor to launch an MVP (minimum viable product) within a 10-week bootcamp setting.

    Through this hands-on approach, you'll master all stages of the product development lifecycle, from ideation and research to launch and iteration. You'll hone proper communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership skills while working closely with your team and mentor.

    Successfully launching an MVP will definitely provide a significant confidence boost and showcases your abilities to potential employers

  • The overall cost of the Sprintleap bootcamp is $600. To make the program more accessible, we offer flexible installment plans, allowing you to spread the payment over two installments. This means you can pay $300 initially and $300 later.

    Currently, we are not offering scholarships for the Sprintleap bootcamp. However, we are actively exploring options to introduce financial aid programs in the future.

  • By the end of the bootcamp, you'll have a solid understanding of the product development lifecycle and the skills needed to be a successful product manager.
    You'll also have a portfolio of real-world projects to showcase your skills to potential employers. You'll also have a network of product managers and mentors to help you grow in your career.

  • Sprintleap bootcamp is for junior product managers who wants to grow by building real-world products with a mentor. It's also for people who are looking to transition into product management from other fields. You'll learn by doing and build a real-world MVP from scratch.

    We advise you take our product management introductory course before applying for the bootcamp. This will help you understand the basics of product management and help you decide if product management is right for you.

  • The application process is simple and straightforward. You'll need to fill out a short application form and submit it. We'll review your application and set up an interview with you. The interview is a 30-minute video call where we'll get to know you better and answer any questions you may have about the program.

    If you're accepted into the program, you'll be paired with a team of engineers, designers, and a senior PM mentor. You'll also be added to a Slack channel where you'll meet your team and mentor.

  • The projects are real-world products that solve real-world problems. You'll work with your team and mentor to build an MVP (minimum viable product) that solves a specific problem.
    We have tonnes of ideas and problems to solve. You can also bring your own ideas to the table.

  • The mentors will guide and support junior PMs by providing feedback and answering questions. They'll also help junior PMs navigate the product development lifecycle and provide career advice.

  • We have a Slack channel where you'll meet your team and mentor. You'll also have access to a community of product managers and mentors. You can ask questions, share ideas, and collaborate with other product managers in the community.

  • We offer career support by helping you build a portfolio of real-world projects. We'll also help you with your resume and LinkedIn profile. We'll also provide career advice and help you prepare for interviews.
